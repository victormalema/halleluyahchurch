Who We Are

Our Story of 33 Years

Our Mission

To love God passionately, serve people intentionally, and make disciples who transform communities with the power of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To be a Spirit-filled church that equips every believer to live their God-given calling — building a legacy of faith that outlasts generations.

Our Values

Scripture, prayer, authentic worship, radical generosity, compassion for the broken, and unwavering commitment to the Great Commission.

Our Reach

With 30 branch churches and over 3,000 members, Halleluyah Church is a growing family committed to touching every corner of our nation with hope.

Our Journey

33 Years of God's Faithfulness

From a small gathering to a movement — every chapter of our story is written in God's grace.

1991

The Beginning

Halleluyah Church was founded with a small congregation of 40 committed believers, meeting in a rented hall. The vision was simple: a church that would be a genuine home for anyone seeking God.

1998

First Branch Church

Seven years in, the congregation had grown so dramatically that the first daughter church was planted — a milestone that marked the beginning of Halleluyah's multi-church vision.

2005

Permanent Sanctuary

The church acquired and built its permanent home — a sanctuary to seat over 2,000 worshippers. What had once seemed impossible now stood as a testimony to God's provision.

2012

10 Branch Churches

A decade of multiplication saw Halleluyah establish 10 branch churches, each serving their local community with the same spirit of faith and excellence as the mother church.

2020

Resilience Through Trials

Through global challenges, Halleluyah Church pivoted rapidly to digital ministry — reaching more people online than ever before, demonstrating that the church is a people, not just a building.

2024

30 Branch Churches & Growing

Today, 33 years from that first gathering, Halleluyah Church stands as a family of 30 branch churches and over 3,000 members — and we believe the best chapters are still being written.

Founding & Presiding Bishop

A Heart That Welcomes You

Grace and peace to you. Whether you are visiting our website for the very first time — searching for a place to belong exploring your faith , or simply curious about who we are, i want to personaly welcome you.it is an honor to have you here."

"Our church was founded on one simple but life-changing truth: God loves every person, and His doors are always open. Regardless of your background, your story, or where you are in life's journey, you are welcome here.".

What began in faith has become a family. Thirty branch churches. Thousands of lives transformed. Marriages restored. Children raised in the fear of God. Communities impacted. And we are only just beginning. This is not my story — it is God's story, and you are invited into the next chapter.

Whether you are new to faith or have walked with God for decades, I want you to know — there is a seat at this table for you. Come as you are. You will not leave the same.

Dr. Zablon Amuliodo Malema
Founding & Presiding Bishop — Halleluyah Church International
